This is the introduction remarks I want to insert: These cats have become more than just animals at KISTI; they are friends and companions to the people working there. They bring joy, comfort, and sometimes even inspiration to the KISTI community. Feel free to visit and enjoy the delightful company of our KISTI cats!

Leon: Known as the most adorable cat at KISTI, Leon is a friendly and playful cat loved by all who meet her.

Jeotso (Milk Cow): Cute and cuddly, Jeotso has a special fondness for churrs. His unique appearance has earned him the nickname "Milk Cow."

Lucy: Lucy, known as Jeotso's lover, follows him everywhere. Her loyal and affectionate nature has endeared her to many.

Leo: The mother of Leon, Leo spends her time near her daughter, nurturing and protecting her.

JJobso (Fake Milk Cow): Shy yet vocal, JJobso bears a striking resemblance to Jeotso but is easily distinguishable by her timid nature.

Gateau: An all-black cat with a preference for sleep, Gateau's relaxed and calm demeanor is a comforting presence at KISTI.
